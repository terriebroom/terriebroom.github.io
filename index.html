The Mid Autumn Bloom

Autumn Breeze

Hello and welcome! This is a story of the Mid Autumn Bloom. Born in warm mid November, Bloom was made to be an early bloomer.

Growing up, she learned very quickly and mastered the visual and performing arts.

She would re-create images of her favourite comic characters, and drum up fictiscious characters from her vivid and child-like imagination.

Bloom also has a Blog where she shares her thoughts and feelings she experiences at a particular point in time.

The Bloom Takes Flight

Travelling is a favourite hobby of Bloom's, meeting new people and learning about new cultures. So far, she has lived and worked in three countries.

Bloom enjoys experiencing new foods and participating in various cultural activities in the countries she visits. Her favourite food experience is sushi in SOHO, New York.
